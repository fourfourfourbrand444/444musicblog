FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before submitting your music.

How soon will my campaign go live?
Your campaign goes live within 24 hours of submission and payment. We review every track to make sure it's ready for our playlists. You'll receive a confirmation once you're live.
Which genres do you accept?
We accept all genres: Afrobeats, Highlife, Gospel, Hip-Hop, Amapiano, Reggae, Dancehall, R&B, Drill, and more. If it's African music, we promote it.
Do I need a distributor to submit?
No! You can submit with a direct link — Audiomack, YouTube, SoundCloud or any streaming link. However, to appear on Spotify and Boomplay playlists, your track should already be distributed to those platforms.
Are these real streams or fake plays?
100% real. We promote to our genuine followers and playlist listeners. Zero bots, zero fake streams. We believe in organic, authentic growth that benefits your career long-term.
How do I pay for the promotion?
After submitting your details, you'll be directed to payment options including Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo) and bank transfer. Payment confirms your slot.
Can I promote more than one song?
Yes! Each song is treated as a separate campaign at ₵99 per song. Many artists submit multiple tracks and see compounding growth across their catalogue.
